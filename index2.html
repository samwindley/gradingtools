Giving students a voice when creating teams

In lieu of a final exam, the conclusion of the course (Practical English IV) would be a "team presentation". The first step

The first step was to ask students questions about their presentation grading preferences, such as which aspects of a presentation they think should affect their grade, and who they trust to give them a fair grade.

Students were already familiar with Kahoot! so we used a Kahoot! quiz to pose the questions. We asked students about the importance of the following:

  • preparation
  • slides
  • speaking loudly and clearly
  • presentation length
  • being entertaining
  • choosing an interesting topic
